## 2. What VenderScope Is

VenderScope is a **passive vendor risk intelligence platform**. It aggregates publicly available security signals — breach records, CVE databases, infrastructure exposure data, and publicly accessible compliance documents — to generate indicative risk scores for third-party vendors.

### Important Limitations

**VenderScope is not:**
- A certified security audit tool
- A penetration testing service
- A legal compliance certification service
- A guarantee of a vendor's actual security posture

**Risk scores are indicative only.** They are derived from public data sources which may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate. A low score does not mean a vendor is secure; a high score does not mean a vendor is negligent. VenderScope scores should be one input into a broader due diligence process, not a substitute for it.
